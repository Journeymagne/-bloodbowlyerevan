| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2 | 1 | Morning Dew | -1 to Rush tests. -1 to all ball pick-up tests. |

| 4-10 | 3-4 | Normal Weather | No additional effect. |
| 11 | 5 | Foggy Morning | Players' MA cannot exceed 6. Only Quick and Short passes are allowed. |
