## Advancement

Players earn SPP and spend it to gain skills according to the base game rules.

Player value increases as follows:

- Primary skill: +20k
- Secondary skill: +40k
- AV: +10k
- PA: +20k
- MA: +30k
- AG: +40k
- ST: +50k

## Elite Combinations

Elite skill combinations increase a player's value by an additional 15k if they were gained through advancement.

- Claws + Mighty Blow: +15k
- Guard + Defensive: +15k
- Wrestle + Evasive: +15k
