## Rule summary
This player may make a Stab Special Action against an adjacent standing opponent. Make an unmodified Armour roll; if armour breaks, roll Injury. It can replace the Block in a Blitz, but then the activation ends.

Full reference: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Stab: Removed from the skill list; it is now a trait, as it was before.
