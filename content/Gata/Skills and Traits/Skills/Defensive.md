## Rule summary
During the opponent's turn, opposing players Marked by this player cannot use Guard or Put the Boot In. In Gata League, Defensive also cancels Offensive.

Full reference: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Defensive: Now cancels the Offensive skill (functionally unchanged).
