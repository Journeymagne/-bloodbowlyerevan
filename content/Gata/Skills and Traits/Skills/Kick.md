## Rule summary
If this player is the nominated kicker, the coach may reduce kick deviation from D6 squares to D3 squares.

Full reference: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Kick: Changed. Instead of a d6 roll, halve the d6 value during kickoff, rounding down. The final value can be 0.
