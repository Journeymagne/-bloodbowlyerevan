## Rule summary
This player may make a Chomp Special Action against a standing opponent they Mark. On 3+, the opponent is Chomped and cannot leave while still Marked by this player. Strip Ball also cannot be used against this player.

Full reference: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Monstrous Mouth: Allows holding no more than 1 player. Exception: if used with Multiple Block, then up to 2 can be held.
