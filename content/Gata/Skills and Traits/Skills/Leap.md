## Rule summary
During movement, this player may Leap over one adjacent square. Resolve it like a Jump, but reduce the negative modifier by 1, to a minimum of -1. This skill cannot be combined with Pogo.

Full reference: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Leap: Now always works on an unmodifiable 4+ roll (improved by Very Long Legs mutation).
