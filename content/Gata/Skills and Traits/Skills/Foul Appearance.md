## Rule summary
An opponent that declares a Block Action or direct Special Action against this player must roll first. On a failed roll, the action is cancelled and the opponent's activation ends.

Full reference: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Foul Appearance: Now triggers on a 1-2 and does not end the player's activation upon triggering.
